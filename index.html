Welcome

Fellowship Association of Chinese Students and ScholarsFACSS is a nonprofit organization that seeks to promote Chinese culture and serve Chinese students on-campus. There are various cultural events on campus all year long such as Moon Festival and New Year Celebration.We are also looking at providing more services for new students such as airport pickup and temporary accommodation. Seminars on different topics will be available, as we get outside school resources involved. In a word, we strive to do things that are beneficial for all the Chinese.

Ceremony
